Chocolate Chip Cookies


Ingredients:
  • 1 cup bread flour
  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp espresso powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 bar semi sweet chocolate
  • 1 bar dark chocolate

Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven at 350 degrees.
  2. Brown the 1 cup of butter, over medium heat.

    3. Do not overstir or burn the butter.

  3. Take the brown butter off the heat an set aside to cool.

    4. Place in fridge or set it aside, do not put in freezer or add ice.

  4. Sift the bread flour, all purpose flour, baking soda, and kosher salt together into a bowl.
  5. Mix the sifted ingredients together by folding.
  6. Place the white and brown sugar together into a bowl.
  7. Cut the chocolate bars into small chunks (non uniform cuts are preferred).
  8. Place brown butter into mixing bowl, add the sugars to butter and mix.
  9. Then add the espresso powder and vanilla into the bowl.
  10. Drop the egg yolk and egg into the mixed butter and mix.
  11. Slowly begin adding the mixed flour to the bowl and mix well.
  12. Once dough has formed, add the chocolate chunks and fold them in.

    13. Optional: For best flavor, place dough in the fridge and let it rest for 24 hours.

  13. Scoop cookie dough into small round balls and place on tin (do not flatten the balls).
  14. Place tin into oven and cook at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
  15. Enjoy
