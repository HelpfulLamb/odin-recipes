Brownies


Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Frosting Ingredients:
  • 3 tbsp softened butter
  • 3 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup confetioners' sugar

Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven at 350 degrees. Grease and flour an 8 inch square pan.
  2. Melt 1/2 cup butter in a large saucepan over medium heat (do not burn).
  3. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
  4. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  5. Spread batter into prepared pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 25 to 30 minutes.
  7. Let cool briefly before frosting.

    8. Frosting:

  8. Combine softened butter, confectioners' sugar, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract in a bowl.
  9. Stir until smooth.
  10. Frost brownies while they are still warm.
  11. Enjoy
